This Portfolio Management Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of organizational performance across key financial and operational metrics. It is designed with robust time-intelligence capabilities, enabling users to analyze trends and make informed decisions with ease.

The dashboard supports multiple dynamic time filters, including:
- Year-to-Date (YTD)
- Quarter-to-Date (QTD)
- Full Year
- Current Month

With these options, users can seamlessly switch between different reporting periods, monitor performance over time, and identify patterns that support strategic planning and timely intervention.
